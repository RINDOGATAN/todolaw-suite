## How to sign

We accept two forms of certification, and we ask contributors for both:

1. **Per-commit sign-off (DCO).** Certify the origin of each commit by adding a
   `Signed-off-by: Name <email>` line (`git commit -s`). See
   [`DCO.txt`](DCO.txt) for the certificate you are agreeing to.
2. **One-time CLA.** The first time You open a pull request, sign this Agreement
   once. Until an automated CLA assistant is wired up, sign by adding Your name
   to [`AUTHORS`](AUTHORS) in the same PR and writing, in the PR description:

   > I have read the CONTRIBUTOR LICENSE AGREEMENT and I accept it. — Full name, date

**Corporate contributions:** if You are contributing on behalf of an employer,
have an authorised signatory email **legal@rindogatan.com** to execute a
Corporate CLA covering employees You designate.
